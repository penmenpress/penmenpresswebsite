This plugin allows Flytedesk publishers to easily insert digital ad units to be served via the Flytedesk platform.  https://flytedesk.com

== Description ==

This plugin allows registered [Flytedesk](https://flytedesk.com) publishers to easily configure their sites to integrate with the Flytedesk platform for digital ad sales.

You will need your unique publisher or digital property id from your mediakit to complete the configuration.  This 36 character string will
be provided by your Flytedesk contact and is also available by logging into the Flytedesk platform.

After you enable this plugin a new widget type will be available -- Flytedesk Ad Unit.  Adding an ad unit is as simple
as adding the widget to the sections of your site where you wish ads to be displayed, and selecting the ad unit type in the widget's settings.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/flytedesk-digital` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the Settings->Flytedesk screen to enter your unique identifier
1. Add Flytedesk Ad Unit widgets to the sections of your site where you would like ads displayed
